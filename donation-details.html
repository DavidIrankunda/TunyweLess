Donate to Steps Initiative: Make a Difference Today

At the Steps Initiative, we believe in the power of community and the difference that each individual can make. Founded by young people who have faced mental health challenges and substance addiction, our organization is dedicated to raising awareness, fighting stigma, and providing support to those in need. Your generous contributions will directly impact our programs, helping us empower youth and create a supportive environment for mental health and addiction recovery.

Why Your Support Matters

Your donations enable us to:

  • Promote Mental Health Awareness: Fund workshops and educational materials that inform young people about mental health issues and substance abuse.

  • Support Rehabilitation Programs: Provide essential resources for individuals in rehabilitation centers, fostering a sense of community and encouraging sustainable recovery

  • Expand Our Reach: Enhance our media campaigns to engage more individuals in discussions about mental health, breaking down barriers and stigma.


How Your Contribution Helps

  • $50: Supplies educational materials for one workshop, reaching up to 30 adolescents.

  • $100: Supports our outreach efforts to provide resources for individuals in rehabilitation.

  • $500: Funds an entire series of workshops in schools, promoting mental well-being among hundreds of students.

  • $1000: Enables us to launch a media campaign that raises awareness on a larger scale, reaching thousands in the community.

Join us in creating change by investing in the Steps Initiative, where your support directly impacts the future of Rwanda’s youth. Together, we can build a society that understands mental health, prevents addiction, and empowers every young person to thrive. Ready to make a difference? Visit our donation page to contribute today or contact us for partnership opportunities. Every contribution counts and brings us closer to creating hope and saving lives. Thank you for your support!
